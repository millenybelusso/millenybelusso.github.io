Privacy Policy


Bryan’s Café is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website’s enquiry form.

When you submit an enquiry through our website, we may collect the following information:

Personal Information: Name, email address, and any other details you voluntarily provide.
Message Content: Any information shared in the enquiry form.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected via the enquiry form for the following purposes:

Responding to Enquiries: To contact you regarding your enquiry and provide information about our services.

3. Data Protection and Security
We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Only authorized personnel have access to the data you provide.

4. Data Sharing
We do not share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law.
